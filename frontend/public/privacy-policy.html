Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 29, 2026  ·  Platform: Android  ·  Last Updated: March 29, 2026

Short version: StayHardy collects only the data needed to run the app. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for advertising or marketing purposes. Ever.

1. Introduction

Welcome to StayHardy ("we", "our", or "us"). StayHardy is a productivity application built for Android that helps you track tasks, habits, and goals and visualize your consistency over time.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your data. By using StayHardy, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

Data Type Examples Purpose
Account Information Email address, display name Create and manage your account, authentication
App Content Tasks, habits, goals, routines, reminders, notes Core app functionality — storing and displaying your data
Profile Data Avatar photo (optional) Personalise your profile
Purchase Information Subscription status, transaction identifiers Manage Pro subscription and in-app purchases
Support Data Feedback messages and category Respond to support requests
Usage Data Session activity, feature usage patterns Improve app performance and experience

We do not collect your precise GPS location, contacts, SMS messages, call logs, or any sensitive device data.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the StayHardy app and its features
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • To process in-app purchases and manage your subscription status
  • To sync your data across sessions and devices
  • To respond to feedback or support requests you submit
  • To monitor and improve app stability and performance
  • To send you app-related notifications (only if you enable them)

We will never use your data for advertising, profiling, or sell it to any third party.

4. Third-Party Services

StayHardy uses a small number of carefully chosen third-party services to deliver its functionality. Each service has its own privacy policy, linked below.

Supabase
Our backend database and authentication provider. Your account data, tasks, habits, goals, and all app content are stored securely on Supabase servers. Supabase uses industry-standard encryption for data at rest and in transit.
supabase.com/privacy →
RevenueCat
Manages in-app purchases and subscription status for StayHardy Pro. RevenueCat receives your purchase transaction identifiers and subscription status. No payment card details are handled by RevenueCat or StayHardy — payments are processed entirely by Google Play.
revenuecat.com/privacy →
Google Play (Billing)
All payment processing for subscriptions and one-time purchases is handled by Google Play. StayHardy does not store or process any credit card or payment information.
policies.google.com/privacy →

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account from within the app (Settings → Delete Account), all your personal data — including tasks, habits, goals, and profile information — is permanently deleted from our servers.

Anonymised, aggregated usage statistics (which cannot identify you) may be retained for internal analytics after account deletion.

6. Data Security

We take data security seriously. All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Data stored in Supabase is encrypted at rest. Passwords are never stored in plain text.

Your app data is only accessible to you and cannot be viewed by other users.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information from within the app
  • Deletion: Permanently delete your account and all associated data via Settings → Delete Account, or by contacting us
  • Portability: Request an export of your data by contacting us
  • Opt-out: Disable push notifications at any time from your device settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

8. Children's Privacy

StayHardy is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Push Notifications

StayHardy may send local notifications to remind you of tasks, habits, or goals. These are processed entirely on your device. You can disable notifications at any time through your Android device settings.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

For significant changes, we will notify you within the app.
